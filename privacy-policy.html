Privacy Policy — Wolf Store

Last updated: 16 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Wolf Store ("we", "us") handles information when you contact us through Instagram Direct Messages, where an automated assistant helps answer your questions and take orders.

Information we collect

  • Your Instagram-provided identifier and the content of the messages you send us.
  • Order details you choose to share (such as product, name, phone number, and delivery address).

How we use your information

  • To answer your questions about products, prices, availability, and delivery.
  • To take and process orders, and to follow up with you about them.
  • To improve the quality of our customer support.

How we store it

Messages and order information are stored securely in our database solely to provide the service described above. We retain them only as long as needed for customer support and order processing.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share it with service providers that help us operate this service (such as our messaging and database providers) and as required by law.

Your choices

You can stop messaging us at any time. To request access to or deletion of your information, contact us using the details below.

Contact

For any privacy questions, contact Wolf Store via Instagram direct message (@jhgt78908ihg) or by email at the address provided on our profile.
